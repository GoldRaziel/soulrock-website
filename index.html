About the Soul Rock Museum Restaurant

Soul Rock is not just a restaurant – it’s an immersive experience that blends food, cinema, music, and emotion. Enter a place where the spirit of the 80s and 90s lives on through full-scale statues, iconic memorabilia, rock legends, and holograms of music and movie heroes. All set in a modern, elegant, and electrifying atmosphere in downtown Dubai.
